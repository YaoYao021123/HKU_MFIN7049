MFIN7049 Study Review

This is a static HTML review site for HKU MFIN7049. The material is organized by topic modules, starting from the big picture of the financial system and moving into interest rates, valuation, risk pricing, information frictions, financing incentives, bank runs, financial crises, and monetary policy.

Each page follows a structured review format: expanded concepts, formula chains, worked examples, interactive questions, feedback, and a bridge to the next module.
